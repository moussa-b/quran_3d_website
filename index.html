Immersive reading, a natural gesture

The page curls under your finger — lift, follow, release — with a soft shadow and the next page gently revealed, exactly like paper.

While you read, the page stays perfectly flat and sharp. The curl only appears while turning. Right-to-left reading is fully respected: the mushaf behaves like a real Arabic book.

One tap hides everything. Distraction-free mode gives you the full screen — no bars, no buttons, just the text.

A true mushaf, everywhere

  • Faithful mushaf High-quality pages with color Tajweed, Hafs script. The sacred text is never restyled, recolored or inverted.
  • Direct navigation Jump by surah, hizb, page number or verse — search the 114 surahs by name or number.
  • Offline reading Download a page, a surah, a hizb or the whole Qur'an, then read without any network.

Made for tablets too

On a tablet in landscape, the mushaf opens as a real two-page spread — just like the printed book, with the same faithful Tajweed pages.

No account, no ads, no trackers. Your reading position, bookmarks and settings never leave your device.
