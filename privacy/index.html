Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Quran's Pages is built so that your reading stays yours. The short version: the app collects no personal data at all. Here is the full picture, in plain words.

No personal data is collected

The app requires no account. It never asks for your name, e-mail address, phone number or location, and it has no way to identify you.

No analytics, no trackers, no ads

The app contains no analytics tools, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDK of any kind. Nothing measures, profiles or observes how you use it.

Everything stays on your device

Your reading position, bookmarks, downloaded pages and settings are stored only on your device. They never leave it, and they are deleted when you uninstall the app.

The app's only network activity

The app connects to the internet for a single purpose: downloading the Qur'an page images from the project's server (quran-pages.bdzapps.com) so they can be displayed and cached for offline reading. The server keeps no logs: no IP addresses, no request history, nothing is recorded or stored about who downloads the pages. No personal reading data is ever transmitted.

No sharing, no selling

Since nothing is collected, there is nothing to share or sell — and there never will be anything shared or sold to third parties.

Children

The app does not specifically target children and collects no data about anyone, including children.

Your rights

Because no personal data is collected or held by the publisher, there is no data to access, correct or delete on our side. You keep full control on your device: you can clear downloaded pages in the app's settings, or remove everything by uninstalling the app.

Store declarations

This policy is consistent with the app's Google Play “Data safety” declaration and the Apple App Store “App Privacy” label: Data Not Collected.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Write to contact@quran.bdzapps.com.

Changes

If the app ever changes in a way that affects privacy, this page will be updated and the date above revised. Any evolution of this policy will be published here.
