Frequently asked questions

How do I read offline?

Download what you need first: a single page, a surah, a hizb, or the whole Qur'an. Once downloaded, pages are cached on your device and open without any network. You can manage or delete downloads at any time in Settings → Downloads.

Why does a page ask me to download it?

Page images are fetched on demand to keep the app small. A page you have never opened needs to be downloaded once; after that it is stored on your device and opens instantly, online or offline.

Is the app free?

Yes. The app is free, with no ads and no in-app purchases.

Is any of my data collected?

No. There is no account, no analytics, no trackers and no ads. Your reading position, bookmarks and settings stay on your device only. Read the full privacy policy.
